Frequently Asked Questions

How long are mentoring sessions?

Standard mentoring sessions are 60 minutes long. You can book multiple sessions if you need more time.

What is the cost of mentoring?

Mentoring rates vary by mentor. Most sessions range from $50-$150 per hour depending on the mentor's experience level.

How do sessions take place?

Sessions are conducted via video call. You'll receive a link after booking your session.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your session without any penalty.
